Education for All is a global organization that aims to give every child the education they deserve. We provide global and regional leadership in education, strengthen education systems worldwide, and respond to contemporary global challenges through education with gender equality as an underlying principle.


We believe that every child deserves to learn more about the world around them in a safe and engaging environment that molds them to be responsible future members of society. We hope to decrease social problems such as poverty and crime by thoroughly taking care of and providing opportunities to our future generation. Our organization aims to provide quality education for all because of our belief that a brighter tomorrow starts with education for all.
